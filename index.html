Capsule Wardrobes Help the Environment


"Clse-up of Clothes Hanging on a Clothing Rack" by Los Muertos Crew. May 9, 2021. Source: Pexels

Fast fashion has led consumers to buy clothing made from cheap materials that last one season. To increase sustainable shopping, building a capsule wardrobe and investing in high quality peices will help to reduce the toxic waste that comes from impulse buying and tossing clothes when a quick trend runs its course.
