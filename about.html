What Is a Capsule Wardrobe?


"Clse-up of Clothes Hanging on a Clothing Rack" by Los Muertos Crew. May 9, 2021. Source: Pexels

A capsule wardrobe is a small collection of quality clothing items that can be styled and worn in many different ways. Capsule wardrobes make getting dressed easier and less stressful by focusing on pieces that work well together. Creating a capsule closet helps save money and encourages people to wear items they already own instead of buying cheap clothing to only wear once.
